Recipe : Masala Omelette
    **Ingredients**
        * 2 eggs
        * 1 small onion (finely chopped)
        * 1 green chili (chopped)
        * 2 tbsp coriander leaves
        * Salt to taste
        * 1 tsp oil
    **Steps**
        1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
        2. Add chopped onion, green chili, coriander, and salt.
        3. Beat the mixture well with a fork.
        4. Heat oil in a pan on medium flame.
        5. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
        6. Cook for 2–3 minutes until the bottom sets.
        7. Flip the omelette and cook the other side for 1 minute.
        8. Serve hot with bread or chapati.

Recipe : Vegetable Upma
    **Ingredients**
        * 1 cup semolina (rava)
        * 1 small onion (chopped)
        * 1 carrot (chopped)
        * 2 tbsp peas
        * 1 tsp mustard seeds
        * 2 cups water
        * 1 tbsp oil
        * Salt to taste
    **Steps**
        1. Dry roast semolina in a pan for 3–4 minutes and keep aside.
        2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
        3. Add chopped onions and sauté until soft.
        4. Add carrots and peas and cook for 2 minutes.
        5. Pour water and add salt, bring it to a boil.
        6. Slowly add roasted semolina while stirring continuously.
        7. Cook for 3–4 minutes until the mixture thickens.
        8. Serve hot.

Recipe : Dal Tadka
    **Ingredients**
        * 1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal)
        * 1 tomato (chopped)
        * 1 tsp cumin seeds
        * 2 garlic cloves (chopped)
        * 1 tsp turmeric powder
        * 1 tbsp ghee
        * Salt to taste
    **Steps**
        1. Wash the lentils and pressure cook with turmeric and water for 3 whistles.
        2. Mash the cooked dal slightly.
        3. Heat ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds.
        4. Add garlic and sauté until golden.
        5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
        6. Pour this tempering over the cooked dal.
        7. Add salt and simmer for 3–4 minutes.
        8. Serve with rice or roti.

Recipe : Aloo Fry
    **Ingredients**
        * 3 potatoes (cubed)
        * 1 tsp mustard seeds
        * 1 tsp chili powder
        * 1 tsp turmeric powder
        * 2 tbsp oil
        * Salt to taste
    **Steps**
        1. Heat oil in a pan.
        2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
        3. Add potato cubes and mix well.
        4. Add turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
        5. Cook on medium flame for 10–12 minutes.
        6. Stir occasionally until potatoes become crispy.
        7. Serve hot with rice or chapati.

Recipe : Tomato Rice
    **Ingredients**
        * 2 cups cooked rice
        * 2 tomatoes (chopped)
        * 1 onion (chopped)
        * 1 tsp mustard seeds
        * 1 tsp cumin seeds
        * 1 tbsp oil
        * Salt to taste
    **Steps**
        1. Heat oil in a pan.
        2. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
        3. Add onions and sauté until golden.
        4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
        5. Add salt and cook until the mixture thickens.
        6. Add cooked rice and mix well.
        7. Cook for 2 minutes while stirring gently.
        8. Serve hot.

Recipe : Chicken Fry
    **Ingredients**
        1. 500 g chicken (cut into pieces)
        2. 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
        3. 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
        4. 1 tsp chili powder
        5. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
        6. 1 tsp garam masala
        7. 2 tbsp oil
        8. Curry leaves (optional)
        9. Salt to taste

    **Steps**
        1. Clean and wash the chicken pieces thoroughly.
        2. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions.
        3. Sauté onions until golden brown.
        4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
        5. Add turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
        6. Add chicken pieces and mix well with the spices.
        7. Cook on medium heat until the chicken is tender and slightly crispy.
        8. Sprinkle garam masala and curry leaves and fry for 2 more minutes.
        9. Serve hot.

Recipe : Chicken Biryani
    **Ingredients**
        * 500 g chicken
        * 2 cups basmati rice
        * 2 onions (thinly sliced)
        * 2 tomatoes (chopped)
        * 2 tbsp yogurt
        * 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
        * 1 tsp chili powder
        * 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
        * 1 tsp biryani masala
        * 2 tbsp oil or ghee
        * 4 cups water
        * Salt to taste

    **Steps**
        1. Wash and soak basmati rice for about 20 minutes.
        2. Heat oil or ghee in a pot and fry sliced onions until golden.
        3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
        4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
        5. Add chicken, yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
        6. Cook the chicken for about 5–7 minutes.
        7. Add soaked rice, biryani masala, and water.
        8. Mix gently and cook until the rice is fully done.
        9. Serve hot with raita.

Recipe : Fish Fry
    **Ingredients**
        * 4 fish pieces (any firm fish)
        * 1 tsp chili powder
        * 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
        * 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
        * 1 tsp lemon juice
        * 2 tbsp oil
        * Salt to taste

    **Steps**
        1. Clean and wash the fish pieces properly.
        2. In a bowl, mix chili powder, turmeric, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and salt.
        3. Apply the spice mixture evenly on the fish pieces.
        4. Marinate the fish for 15–20 minutes.
        5. Heat oil in a pan.
        6. Place the marinated fish pieces in the pan.
        7. Fry each side for about 3–4 minutes until crispy and cooked well.
        8. Remove from the pan and serve hot.
