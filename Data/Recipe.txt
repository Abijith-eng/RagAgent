Recipe : Paneer Butter Masala
    **Ingredients**
        * 200 g paneer (cubed)
        * 2 tomatoes (pureed)
        * 1 onion (chopped)
        * 1 tbsp butter
        * 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
        * 1 tsp garam masala
        * 2 tbsp cream
        * Salt to taste
    **Steps**
        1. Heat butter in a pan.
        2. Add onions and sauté until golden.
        3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
        4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
        5. Add salt and garam masala.
        6. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
        7. Add cream and mix well.
        8. Serve hot.

Recipe : Egg Fried Rice
    **Ingredients**
        * 2 cups cooked rice
        * 2 eggs
        * 1 onion (chopped)
        * 1 tbsp soy sauce
        * 1 tbsp oil
        * Salt to taste
    **Steps**
        1. Heat oil in a pan.
        2. Scramble the eggs and set aside.
        3. Add onions and sauté.
        4. Add cooked rice and mix well.
        5. Add soy sauce and salt.
        6. Add scrambled eggs and mix.
        7. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
        8. Serve hot.

Recipe : Chapati
    **Ingredients**

        * Salt to taste
    **Steps**

        2. Add water and knead dough.
        3. Rest for 15 minutes.

        7. Apply ghee if needed.
        8. Serve hot.
